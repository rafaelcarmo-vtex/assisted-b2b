**1\. UX, AI and Writing**

The design and development of our products must follow established, industry-recognized guidelines to ensure clarity, usability, accessibility, and consistency across experiences.

1. For Artificial Intelligence, UX, and Writing, we adopt the Google Material Design guidelines, leveraging their principles for responsible AI behavior, clear user interactions, and concise, human-centered communication. These guidelines help ensure that AI-driven experiences are transparent, predictable, and supportive, while maintaining high standards of usability and content quality.  
2. The look and feel of our products should be minimalist, functional, and intentional, prioritizing clarity over decoration. Visual decisions must be guided by the Apple Human Interface Guidelines, which emphasize simplicity, focus, and deference to content. Layouts, spacing, typography, and interactions should feel natural, refined, and unobtrusive, allowing users to accomplish tasks efficiently without cognitive overload.  
3. Text must follow standard UX writing conventions, using sentence case and proper capitalization for sentences and proper nouns.  
4. Avoid all-caps text and system-like placeholders that resemble raw data (e.g. “USER NAME”, “EMAIL ADDRESS”).

Together, these guidelines establish a cohesive foundation for experiences that are intuitive, accessible, and consistent across platforms, while balancing functional rigor with visual elegance.
